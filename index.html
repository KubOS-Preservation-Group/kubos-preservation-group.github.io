About the Community

We are a community of enthusiasts interested in the KubOS microsatellite operating system.

KubOS is an open-source Linux-based microsatellite operating system designed for use in space. We hope to provide a space where KubOS enthusiasts can tap into the knowledge and experience of other KubOS users and developers to help build an inclusive community of people and software to help make it easy for anyone to conduct projects in space.

if this sounds interesting to you, come join our discord community. We have some exciting plans for ambitious projects to revisit projects like KubOS Hopper and expand hardware support for KubOS. If you're interested in the future of KubOS, you can also keep an eye on this website's GitHub repository so you dont miss any new announcements or changes.

We're still working on getting things up and running, but here's a fun little piece of KubOS history. Back in 2017, the then-CEO of KubOS did an interview where he talked about a series of pictures that NASA took of a cubesat that he had been holding and working on just months before. One of those very NASA pictures is currently being used as the background image for this website. You can read the mini-writeup about this story and see the full photo series here.
